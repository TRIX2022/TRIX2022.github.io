As a party delegate, you can participate in certain spaces in the agenda (see the agenda for party delegates). The spaces are not mandatory for party delegates, you have free time during the day, you can participate to the sessions mentioned, you can hang out with other party delegates, you can sleep or you can visit the Bran Castle. We know you like to party and have fun, so only parties are mandatory for party delegates. However, if you want to participate to the sessions, you can, but if you would like to share, ask questions etc, please be mindful about it and let the delegates - active members of LC Cluj-Napoca, share or ask their questions first, since the sessions are especially made for them.
